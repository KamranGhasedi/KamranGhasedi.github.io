Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Pittsburgh University. My supervisor is Dr. Heng Huang, and my main research interest lies in the field of Machine Learning and Big Data Mining. In particular, I focus on developing efficient unsupervised, semi-supervised and weakly supervised learnings algorithms on different applications.
